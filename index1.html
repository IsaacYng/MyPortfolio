My Story

I'm Ishak Yonghang (Limbu), born on October 26, 2005 in Nepal. I'm a Christian, originally from Hilihang‑5, and currently based in Phidim town. My journey has taken me from completing my schooling with strong academic results to gaining hands-on experience as a Sales Consultant at a reputed Bajaj Showroom. I believe in continuous learning — having completed computer training courses to stay sharp in today's digital world. Beyond professional life, I find joy in writing poetry, which keeps my creative and emotional intelligence alive.
